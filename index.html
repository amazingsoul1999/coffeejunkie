House Blend Coffee

We bring to you the freshest Coffee seeds sourced from the best plantations in India. Our Coffee seeds are vacuum packed immediately after manufacturing at the garden itself to give you the freshest glass of Chai.
Staying true to our Clean Earth practices, our Coffees are packed in pouches that are 100% biodegradable. A custom designed steel scoop is placed in each pouch to make sure that every scoop has just the desired amount of Coffee in it.
Our endeavor is to become a part of the Chai ritual in your home everyday!
